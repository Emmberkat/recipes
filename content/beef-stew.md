---
layout: recipe
title: "Beef Stew"

params:
    ingredients:
    - Red wine (red blend ideal, but whatever red is on sale works)
    - Beef broth
    - Mirepoix
    - Garlic (paste, fresh, jar whatever)
    - Beef stew meat 1.5 to 2lbs
    - Salt and Peppa
    - Yukon or baby potatoes
    - Tomato paste
    - Ranch seasoning
    - Hot sauce
    - Wash your sister sauce
    - Paprika
    - Beef blend herbs

    directions:
    - Season beef with salt and pepper and sear over high heat in pot (not long time, just for a crust)
    - Remove beef and set aside
    - Add garlic, tomato paste, paprika and ranch seasoning with a splash of wine and broth and saute for afew minutes.
    - Add in Mirepoix and saute with spices till onions are soft, add a few more splashes of wine and broth to keep wet.
    - Add half of remaining wine and broth, then return the meat to the pot, add in the beef blend herbs and hot sauce and wash your sister sauce.
    - Bring to a light boil.
    - Add in cleaned potatoes and remaining wine and broth to keep everything covered.
    - Put lid on pot and let sit over medium low heat for as long as you want, add water if it reduces too much. Serve after at least an hour.
    - Alternatively you can also add a gravy packet or cornstarch slury to thicken more
---
