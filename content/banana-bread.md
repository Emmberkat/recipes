---
layout: recipe
title: "Banana Bread"

params:
    ingredients:
    - 3 cups flour
    - 2 cups sugar
    - 1.5 tsp baking soda
    - 1 tsp salt
    - 3 mashed bananas
    - 3/4 cup vegetable oil
    - 1 tbsp vinegar
    - 1 tbsp vanilla
    - 1 cup cold water

    directions:
    - Combine in 3 9x13 in pans
    - Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes
---
