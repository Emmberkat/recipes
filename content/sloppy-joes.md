---
layout: recipe
title: "Sloppy Joes"

params:
    ingredients:
    - 1 lb ground beef
    - 1 cup ketchup
    - 1 tbsp mustard
    - 1/4 cup brown sugar (packed tight)

    directions:
    - Brown beef in skillet
    - Combine ketchup, mustard, and brown sugar
    - Let cook for an additional minute before serving
---
